Guidelines

In order for us to have a fully trustworthy transaction, I have established some small guidelines I expect you to follow when working with me! Thanks!

  • Respect: This is a huge must-have. Insults, harrassment, or any other offensive method of abuse is NOT tolerated.
  • You Pay First: As standard business practice goes, you must send me my payment before I send you the asset. In order to ensure the trust and safety of both of us, I can send any proof of development you require, thanks! This is not up for negotiation.
  • Deadlines: If you have a deadline, tell me it before I begin developing.
  • Payment clarification: Before I begin development, we must negotiate the pricing. I will not begin development until a fixed price is determined.
  • No excessive edits: While I am happy to make some changes to fully match an asset to your intended quality/liking, if you request an excessive amount of edits I will up the price of the asset. This value is subject to change, but as of the present, you are capped at 3 small edits.
  • References/Detailed Descriptions: I would appreciate reference images and/or descriptions of your intended asset(s).
  • Time Wasting: if you waste my time, I will just block you. It wastes both of our time, just don't do it.
  • Scamming: Obviously, any attempt to scam me or steal my work without proper payment will result in a block, and I will report you.
  • Be Somewhat Active: If you are hiring me, please do not go inactive for prolonged periods of time. I do not want to be waiting for you to reply back to me. I fully undertand that things come up, but please tell me in advance - thanks!
  • No Competing Developers: When hiring me, I do not wish to compete against other developers for the role. If you choose to hire me, I expect only me to be developing on the task (unless you have a team of course, that's understadable).
